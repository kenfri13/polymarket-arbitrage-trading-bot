## 🧩 Troubleshooting

Use this section to fix common issues.

- **Bot does not start:** Check your internet status. Ensure you have the latest version of Windows installed.
- **No trades found:** Verify your profit settings. Your threshold might be too high. Check if the markets have enough volume.
- **Connection errors:** Restart the application. Check your connection to the Polygon network.
- **High gas fees:** Wait for network activity to lower. Polygon gas prices fluctuate throughout the day.
- **Login fails:** Check your credentials. Copy and paste them to avoid typing errors.

## 📜 Understanding Arbitrage

Arbitrage is a simple concept. You find an item trading at two different prices. You buy the item in the cheaper market. You sell the item in the expensive market. You keep the difference as profit. This tool does this task automatically. It executes orders faster than a human can. Speed is important in prediction markets.

## 📂 Folder Structure

When you install the bot, it creates several folders on your computer.

- **Logs:** This folder keeps a history of your trades. Use it to check why a trade failed or succeeded.
- **Config:** This folder holds your individual settings. Do not modify these files unless directed by support.
- **Data:** This folder stores market information for quick access. 
- **Executables:** This folder contains the core files the bot needs to run.

## 💡 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the bot store my private keys?**
The bot saves your keys locally on your machine. They stay on your disk. They do not travel to external servers.

**Can I run multiple instances?**
You should run one instance at a time. Multiple instances might cause conflicts with your account.

**Is there a cost to run this?**
You pay network gas fees for every trade. The software itself is free to use.

**What happens if the power goes out?**
The bot stops instantly. Existing orders might still fill on the blockchain. Check your Polymarket account manually if the power goes out.

**How do I view my profit?**
The dashboard shows your current balance and recent earnings. You can also view your transaction history on a blockchain explorer.

**Why did my trade fail?**
Most failures happen because the market price changed while the order was in transit. This is common in fast markets. The bot handles these cases automatically.
